About Us

October 17, 2018

Always strive to produce professional graduates who have excellence and competitiveness in Literature and/or English, who are built in a Friendly Campus atmosphere, and always develop themselves in a global society. STBA Pontianak is a quality-oriented private university. We always try to produce graduates who have excellence and competitiveness and are ready to develop in a global society. Satisfying customers, namely students is our goal.

Our Goals

October 17, 2018

Produce graduates who have competence in communicating using English according to English rules. To produce graduates who are able to analyze and describe works of English Literature in various genres by using several methods of approaching literary studies and linking them to global sociocultural issues. The implementation of research that supports the teaching and learning process of English Literature, translation and teaching of English. Produce graduates needed by the world of work both locally and globally related to the use of English language teaching skills, literature and translation in various types of suitable jobs. Produce a program of service activities that are beneficial to the general public in accordance with the developed field.

History

October 15, 2018
